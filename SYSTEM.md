## WEEKLY DUNGEONS

> "A dungeon gate has appeared."

Every Saturday, you face a themed dungeon. These are longer sessions (~30-45 min focused work).

### E-Rank Dungeons (Weeks 1-4)

**Dungeon: Goblin Cave**
- 5 rounds of: 10 wall push-ups → 10 squats → 30s skipping → 30s rest
- Clear time target: Under 15 minutes

### D-Rank Dungeons (Weeks 5-8)

**Dungeon: Demon Castle - Floor 1**
- Tabata style: 20s work / 10s rest, 8 rounds
- Exercises: Squats, Knee push-ups, High knees, Plank hold
- 3 sets total with 2 min rest between sets

### C-Rank Dungeons (Weeks 9-14)

**Dungeon: Red Gate**
- AMRAP (As Many Rounds As Possible) in 20 minutes:
- 10 push-ups → 15 squats → 10 lunges → 1 min skip rope → 30s plank
- Track rounds completed. Beat your previous record.

### B-Rank Dungeons (Weeks 15-22)

**Dungeon: Demon Castle - Floor 100**
- 30-minute circuit, no scheduled rest (rest when needed):
- 15 push-ups → 20 squats → 15 lunges → 10 burpees → 2 min rope → 1 min plank
- Repeat until 30 minutes

### A-Rank Dungeons (Weeks 23-30)

**Dungeon: Double Dungeon**
- 45-minute endurance challenge:
- 5 min skip rope → 20 push-ups → 25 squats → 20 lunges → 15 burpees → 1.5 min plank
- Repeat 3-4 times, minimal rest

## NUTRITION PROTOCOL (INT STAT)

> "A new skill has been acquired: [Caloric Awareness]"

### Phase 1: Awareness (E-Rank, Weeks 1-4)
- No strict counting. Just LOG what you eat (photo or note).
- Cut sugary drinks completely (replace with water/black coffee/green tea).
- Reduce fried food to max 2x per week.
- Add protein to every meal (eggs, dal, paneer, chicken, fish).

### Phase 2: Structure (D-Rank, Weeks 5-8)
- Start rough calorie tracking (use an app like HealthifyMe or MyFitnessPal).
- Target: ~2000 calories/day.
- Protein target: 80-90g/day.
- Meal timing: No eating after 9 PM.
- Reduce rice/roti portions by 25%.

### Phase 3: Optimization (C-Rank, Weeks 9-14)
- Target: 1800-2000 calories/day.
- Protein target: 90-100g/day.
- Meal prep 2 days in advance.
- Replace one meal with a high-protein, low-carb option.
- Weekly cheat meal allowed (not cheat day).

### Phase 4: Mastery (B-Rank+, Weeks 15+)
- Target: 1700-1900 calories/day.
- Protein target: 100-110g/day.
- Intuitive eating kicks in — you know what 1800 cal looks like.
- One refeed day per week (eat at maintenance ~2200 cal).

### Sample Daily Meal Template (B-Rank):
```
Breakfast (400 cal): 3 eggs + 2 toast OR oats + protein source
Lunch (500 cal): Rice (small) + dal + sabzi + curd + chicken/paneer
Snack (200 cal): Fruits + handful of nuts OR protein shake
Dinner (500 cal): 2 roti + sabzi + dal + salad
Post-dinner (100 cal): Green tea + 10 almonds (if hungry)
Total: ~1700 cal | Protein: ~100g
```

## PENALTY SYSTEM

> "Warning: Failure to complete daily quest will result in penalties."

| Offense | Penalty |
|---------|---------|
| Miss 1 day (no valid reason) | -10 XP, must do 1.5x next day |

### Valid Rest Reasons (No Penalty):
- Illness or injury
- Scheduled rest day (1/week)
- Travel day (but walk quest still applies)
- "Emergency Quest" swap (replace workout with 10,000 steps)

## RULES OF THE SYSTEM

1. **The System does not negotiate.** Daily quests are non-negotiable (except valid rest reasons).
2. **Progress over perfection.** 50% of a quest done > 0% done. Partial credit exists.
3. **You cannot skip ranks.** Even if weight drops fast, fitness tests must be passed.
4. **Weigh yourself weekly only.** Every Sunday morning, empty stomach. Daily fluctuations are noise.
5. **The System adapts.** If a rank feels too easy for 2 weeks, promote early. If too hard, stay longer (no shame).
6. **No exercise you haven't unlocked.** Stick to your skill tree. Ego lifting equivalent = attempting advanced moves too early = injury.
7. **Rest is a quest.** Sleep and recovery are part of the system, not cheating.
8. **The penalty is real.** Track honestly. The system only works if you respect it.

## EMERGENCY PROTOCOLS

### "I'm too tired today"
→ Do the **Minimum Viable Quest**: 10 squats + 10 push-ups + 1 min plank + walk.
→ Earns 5 XP (reduced, but no penalty).

### "I'm sick/injured"
→ System enters **Rest Mode**. Only hydration + sleep quests active.
→ No XP loss. No streak loss for up to 3 days.
→ Beyond 3 days: streak pauses (not resets).

### "I fell off for a week+"
→ **Re-Awakening Protocol**: Drop back one rank's daily quest for 3 days, then return to current rank.
→ Streak resets. But levels and stats do NOT reset. You keep what you earned.
→ "Even Jinwoo had to restart from the double dungeon."

### "I hit a weight plateau"
→ **System Update**:
  - Increase steps by 2,000/day for 2 weeks
  - Add one extra skip rope session (5 min) on alternate days
  - Check if calories have crept up (re-track strictly for 1 week)
  - Consider a diet break: eat at maintenance for 1 week, then resume deficit
